16 Years Experience

With over a decade and a half of history, our journey has been defined by a relentless pursuit of excellence. From our humble beginnings to becoming a recognized powerhouse on the track, we have consistently evolved our technology and nurtured generations of talented engineers. These sixteen years represent more than just a timeline; they are a testament to our dedication to pushing the boundaries of automotive innovation and student engineering.

513km/l Fuel Efficiency Record

Innovation is at the core of our engineering philosophy. Achieving a staggering record of 513km/l was not a stroke of luck, but the result of countless hours of research, aerodynamic optimization, and precision engine tuning. This milestone stands as a bold statement of our commitment to a sustainable future, proving that through cutting-edge technology and creative problem-solving, we can achieve extreme efficiency to meet the energy challenges of tomorrow.

50+ Championship Titles

Winning is not just a goal; it is our standard. With more than 50 championship titles earned on both national and international stages, we have established a legacy of dominance and consistency. Each trophy reflects the synergy of a dedicated team, a meticulously crafted vehicle, and a winning mindset. We don't just race to compete; we race to set the benchmarks that others strive to follow.
