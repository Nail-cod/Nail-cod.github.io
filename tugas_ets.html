About Us

World Champions in Energy Efficiency

  • 16 Years Experience
  • 513km/l Fuel Efficiency Record
  • 50+ Championship Title

Since 2009, ITS Team Sapuangin has advanced energy-efficient vehicle innovation. Competing in Shell Eco-marathon and Student Formula Japan, we became world champions in London 2018 and continue to represent Indonesia proudly on the global stage.
